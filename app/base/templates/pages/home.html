About Us

"The church that preaches what the Bible teaches."

We are a group of believers whose foundation is anchored on the Word of God, the BIBLE. We firmly believe on the power of the gospel, which is the death, burial and resurrection of our Lord, Jesus Christ, who came to redeem us from our sins with His precious blood that we may obtain eternal life. That JESUS CHRIST ALONE is our only SAVIOR, and no amount of good works can save us from the fires of hell.

We also believe that anyone can come and accept Him as his/her PERSONAL SAVIOUR by faith; for HE alone has the power to transform lives through the power of the HOLY SPIRIT so that one can truly experience abundant and joyful life here on earth.

COME!!! Learn and worship with us and witness the mighty power of God through, JESUS CHRIST alone.
